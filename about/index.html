Background

My heart holds a special place for family nutrition: pregnancy, postpartum, infancy, toddlerhood, and childhood. As a mom of two, I understand how each stage holds unique challenges. My passion is to journey alongside families, teaching simple habit changes that can transform health and give a healthy start to all.

Aside from family nutrition, I love counseling every age and stage, teaching how “all foods can fit” – even as we work to manage weight, improve heart health, reduce diabetes risk, and more. I enjoy communicating that nutrition can be simple and fit every budget. My sessions also include counseling on beginning exercise because I believe it holds an important role in a healthy lifestyle.

As a Registered Dietitian Nutritionist in Nebraska, I am a Licensed Medical Nutrition Therapist. I achieved my Master of Medical Nutrition Degree at University of Nebraska Medical Center. My primary experience has been in public health as a WIC dietitian and certified lactation counselor. I also work at my local YMCA as a group fitness instructor, weight loss program facilitator, and dietitian.

I believe nutrition is a science and I follow research-based practices. Clients drive my work, and I love sprinkling creativity to personalize plans, share recipes, and provide ongoing support. Nutrition and exercise are my hobbies-turned-career, and it is a privilege to serve my community as a dietitian.
